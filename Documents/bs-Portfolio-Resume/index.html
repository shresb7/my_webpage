I am originally from Nepal. Nepal is in South Asia located in between India and China. Have you heard about Mount Everest- the tallest mountain in the world? It's in my homecountry. I was born and brought up there until I decided to come to the United States to pursue my undergraduate degree.

Nepal is a very beautiful country and I am from Kathmandu, the capital. So I was close to all the infrastructures, advanced technology and yes, the crowd. My journey here so far has only been exciting, adventurous and a good learning experience. And I am looking forward to more of it.
