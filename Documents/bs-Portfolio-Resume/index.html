Let's talk more about me

I am originally from Nepal. In case you're wondering- Nepal is a country in South Asia located in between India and China. Have you heard about Mount Everest- the tallest mountain in the world? It's in my homecountry, Nepal. I was born and brought up there until I decided to come to the United States to pursue my undergraduate degree.

Nepal is a very beautiful country and I am from Kathmandu, the capital. So I was close to all the infrastructures, advanced technology and yes, the crowd. In Nepal, we think of US as a very developed country with many facilities, employment opportunities and fine lives. After coming here in 2013, I realized that some of the things that I used to think about the States were right (much developed and advanced, mroe opportunties), however not all. Not all people live high quality life. Living here is not easy. One has to work hard in order to live a better life. My journey here so far has only been exciting, adventurous and a good learning experience. And I am looking forward to more of it.
